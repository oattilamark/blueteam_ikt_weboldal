Lamborghini Aventador LP 780-4 ULTIMAE

The latest version of Aventador is not only powerful, it is the most powerful Aventador ever made. With the highest-performing standard-production naturally aspirated V12 engine in Lamborghini history, it incorporates advanced technological solutions and unparalleled design. This gem, to be produced in only 350 units, is the final—and greatest—expression of a family of super sports cars that has left its mark on the last decade. Aventador LP 780-4 Ultimae is already a classic transformed into an icon.

CONSUMPTION

  • COMBINED CONSUMPTION* 18.0 l/100km (WLTP)
  • COMBINED CO2 EMISSIONS* 442 g/km (WLTP)

WHEELS

  • ELECTRONIC STABILITY CONTROL (ESC): ESC integrating ABS and TCS with different characteristics according to the driving mode selected
  • STEERING: Hydraulic assisted power steering, with 3 different servotronic characteristics coupled with Lamborghini Dynamic Steering (LDS) and Rear Wheel Steering (RWS), managed by drive select mode
  • TYRES – FRONT: New P Zero Corsa 255/30 ZR 20 92Y; P Zero 255/30 ZR 20 92Y available as optional
  • TYRES – REAR: New P Zero Corsa 355/25 ZR 21 107Y; P Zero 355/25 ZR 21 107Y available as optional
  • FRONT RIMS: 9”J X 20”
  • REAR RIMS: 13”JX21”

SUSPENSION

  • SUSPENSION: Push rod magnetorheologic active front and rear suspension with horizontal dampers and springs

BRAKING SYSTEM

  • BRAKES: Carbon-ceramic brakes with fixed monoblock calipers in aluminum with 6 pistons (front) and 4 pistons (rear)

ENGINE

  • ENGINE: V12, 60°, MPI (Multi Point Injection)
  • DISPLACEMENT: 6,498 cm3 (396.5 cu in)
  • BORE AND STROKE: 95 mm x 76.4 mm (3.74 x 3.01 in)
  • COMPRESSION RATIO: 11.8:0.2
  • MAX. POWER: 780 CV (574 kW) at 8,500 rpm
  • MAX. TORQUE: 720 Nm (531 lb.-ft.) @ 6,750 rpm
  • EMISSION CLASS: EURO 6 - LEV 3
  • EMISSION CONTROL SYSTEM: Catalytic converters with lambda sensors
  • COOLING SYSTEM: Water and oil cross flow cooling system with variable air inlets
  • ENGINE MANAGEMENT SYSTEM: Lamborghini Iniezione Elettronica (LIE) with Ion current analysis
  • LUBRICATION SYSTEM: Dry sump

SAFETY

  • AIRBAG: Driver, passenger, side airbags. Knee airbags only for specific markets

TRANSMISSION

  • TRANSMISSION TYPE: Electronically controlled all-wheel drive system (Haldex gen. IV) with rear mechanical self-locking differential
  • GEARBOX: ISR (Independent Shifting Rods) gearbox with 7 speeds
  • CLUTCH: Dry double plate clutch, Ø 235 mm (9.25 in)

PERFORMANCE

  • TOP SPEED: 355 km/h
  • ACCELERATION 0-100 KM/H: 2.8 s
  • ACCELERATION 0-200 KM/H: 8.7 s

DIMENSION

  • WHEELBASE: 2,700 mm (106.30 in)
  • OVERALL LENGTH: 4,868 mm (191.65 in)
  • OVERALL WIDTH (EXCLUDING MIRRORS): 2,098 mm (82.59 in)
  • OVERALL WIDTH (INCLUDING MIRRORS): 2,273 mm (89.48 in)
  • OVERALL HEIGHT: 1,136 mm (44.72 in)
  • FRONT TRACK: 1,720 mm (67.71 in)
  • REAR TRACK: 1,680 mm (66.14 in)
  • DRY WEIGHT*: 1,550 kg (3,417 lb);
  • WEIGHT-TO-POWER RATIO: 1.98 kg/CV (4.36 lb/CV)
  • WEIGHT DISTRIBUTION (FRONT-REAR): 43% - 57%

BODY AND CHASSIS

  • FRAME: Carbon fiber monocoque with aluminium front and rear frames
  • BODY: Engine bonnet, rear air inlets and spoiler in carbon fiber; other panels in aluminum and synthetic material. High pressure RTM carbon fiber Hard Top painted (Aventador S Roadster)
  • MIRRORS: Electrically operated, heated and foldable
  • AERODYNAMICS: Rear electronically operated spoiler with 3 positions; completely panelled underbody
